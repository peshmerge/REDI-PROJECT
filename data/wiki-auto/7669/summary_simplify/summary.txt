A centimetre (international spelling as used by the International Bureau of Weights and Measures; symbol cm) or centimeter (American spelling) is a unit of length in the metric system, equal to one hundredth of a metre, "centi" being the SI prefix for a factor of .
A centimetre is approximately the width of the fingernail of an average adult person.
One millilitre is defined as one cubic centimetre, under the SI system of units.
They are mostly used only with East Asian fixed-width CJK fonts, because they are equal in size to one Chinese character.
