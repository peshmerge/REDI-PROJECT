Pope Anastasius III (; died June 913) was an Italian cleric of the Roman Catholic Church and the 121st Pope from 911 until his death in 913.
Anastasius was born in Rome.
Anastasius was elected pope in 911.
He was pope during a period when Rome and the Papacy were in the power of Theophylact family.
He was active in developing the dioceses of Germany.
He was buried in St. Peter's Basilica.
