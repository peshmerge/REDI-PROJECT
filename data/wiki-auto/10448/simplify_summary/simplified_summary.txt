emperor kogeneux [unk] 元 天 皇 , kogen-tennorth , also known as oyamatonekohikokunikuru no mikoto , was the eighth emperor of japan , according to the traditional order of succession .
very little is known about this emperor .
after his death in 158 bc , one of his sons supposedly became the next emperor .
in the " kojiki " and " nihon shoki " , only kogen 's name and genealogy were recorded .
the imperial " misasagi " or tomb for kogen is currently maintained .
kogen was the eldest son of emperor korei .
one of kogen 's other sons named prince hikofutsuoshinomakoto was also grandfather to legendary japanese hero takenouchi no sukune .
emperor kogen reigned until his death in 158 bc .
the existence of the first nine emperors is not certain .
the name kogen "-tenno " was given to him posthumously by later generations .
this emperor 's official name after his death -lrb- his posthumous name -rrb- was regularized many centuries after the lifetime which was ascribed to kogen .
the actual site of his grave is not known .
outside of the kojiki , the reign of emperor kimmei aarhus 509 – 571 adhesion is the first for which contemporary historiography is able to assign verifiable dates .
the conventionally accepted names and dates of the early emperors were not confirmed as traditional .
