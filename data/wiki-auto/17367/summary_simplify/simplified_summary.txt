bloch received the nobel prize in physiology or medicine in 1964 with feodor lynen paces .
he was the second child of hedwigarded striemerrford and frederich d . " fritz " bloch .
in 1934 , he fled to the " schweizerische forschungsinstitut " in davos , switzerland before moving to the united states in 1936 .
he was appointed to the department of biological chemistry at yale medical school .
he studied at columbia university and got a phd in biochemistry in 1938 .
from there he went to the university of chicago and then harvard university .
after retirement at harvard , he served as the mack and effie campbell tyner eminent scholar chair in the college of human sciences at florida state university .
bloch shared the nobel prize in physiology or medicine with feodor lynen in 1964 .
their work showed that the body first makes squalene from acetate over many steps and then converts the squalene to cholesterol .
he traced all the carbon atoms in cholesterol back to acetate .
this was possible because fungi also produce squalene .
he was one of several researchers who showed acetyl coenzyme a is turned into mevalonic acid .
both bloch and lynen then showed that mevalonic acid is converted into chemically active isoprene , the precursor to squalene .
in 1988 , he was awarded the national medal of science .
bloch met his wife lore teutsch in munich .
