a caesar salad is a green salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice nodes or lime juice ashore .
in its original form , this salad was prepared and served tableside .
the salad is said to have been made by caesar cardini , an italian immigrant who operated restaurants in mexico and the united states .
his daughter rosa said that her father invented the salad at his restaurant caesar 's wristtre at the hotel cesaricum .
in 1946 , newspaper columnist dorothy kilgallen wrote of a caesar containing anchovies , differing from cardini 's version : the big food rage in hollywood — the caesar salad — will be introduced to new yorkers by gilmore 's steak house .
the salad was later renamed to caesar 's salad .
cardini 's daughter said that the original recipe included whole lettuce leaves , which were meant to be lifted by the stem and eaten with the fingers ; coddled eggs ; and italian olive oil .
bottled caesar dressings are now made and marketed by many companies .
the trademark brands " cardini 's " , " caesar cardini 's " and " the original caesar dressing " are all claimed to date to february 1950 , although they were only registered decades later , and more than a dozen varieties of bottled " cardini 's " dressing are available today , with various ingredients .
