the acm a . m . turing award is an annual prize given by the association for computing machinerytious acmhesis to an individual selected for contributions " of lasting and major technical importance to the computer field " .
the turing award is generally recognized as the highest distinction in computer science and the " nobel prize of computing " .
it is named after alan turing , a british mathematician and reader in mathematics at the university of manchester .
turing is often credited as being the key founder of theoretical computer science and artificial intelligence .
the prize was given with an additional prize of $ 250,000 , with financial support provided by intel and google .
since 2014 , the award has been accompanied by a prize of $ 1 million , with financial support provided by google .
the first recipient was alan perlis , of carnegie mellon university .
the first female recipient was frances e . allen of ibm in 2006 .
