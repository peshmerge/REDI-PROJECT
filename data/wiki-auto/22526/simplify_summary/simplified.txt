organometallic chemistry is the study of organic compounds containing at least one carbon atom of an organic molecule and a metal .
these include carbon monoxide , cyanide , or carbide .
some related compounds such as transition metal hydrides and metal phosphine are often included in discussions of organometallic compounds , though strictly speaking , they are not necessarily organometallic .
the term " metalorganic compound " refers to metal-containing compounds lacking direct metal-carbon bonds but which contain organic ligands .
metal beta-diketonates , alkoxides , dialkylamides , and metal phosphine complexes are members of this class .
organometallic chemistry combines aspects of traditional inorganic and organic chemistry .
organometallic compounds are used in research and industrial chemical reactions .
