emperor annei ringo starr 天 皇 , also known as " shikitsuhikotamatemi no mikoto " – was the third emperor of japan , according to the traditional order of succession .
annei is almost certainly a legend .
after his death in 511 bc , his second or third son supposedly became the next emperor .
emperor annei 's name appears in both the " kojiki " and " nihon shoki " where only his genealogy is recorded .
before he became the monarch , he was known as prince shikitsu-hiko tamatemi .
during emperor annei 's legend , he had one wife named " nunasoko-nakatsu-hime " and fathered three children with her .
his second or third son then took the throne and would later be referred to as emperor itoku .
historians consider details about the life of emperor annei to be a legendary emperor .
this emperor 's official name after his death -lrb- his posthumous name -rrb- was regularized many centuries after the lifetime which was ascribed to annei .
the actual site of his grave is not known .
the imperial household agency designates this location as annei 's mausoleum .
the conventionally accepted names and dates of the early emperors were not confirmed as traditional .
