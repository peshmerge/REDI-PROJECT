hexameter is a metrical line of verse .
it was the standard epic metre in classical greek and latin literature , such as in the " odyssey " and " aeneid " .
it was used in other genres of composition including horace 's satires , ovid 's " metamorphoses " and the hymns of orpheus .
hexameter was invented by phemonoe , the daughter of apollo and the first pythia of delphi .
a short syllable is a syllable with a short vowel and no consonant at the end .
a long syllable syllable is a syllable that either has a long vowel , one or more consonants at the end aarhus or a long consonanthesis .
spaces between words are not counted in syllabification , so for instance " cat " is a long syllable in isolation , but " cat attack " would be syllabified as short-long : " ca " , " ta " , " tack " υ-bundesliga " .

in the 17th century the iambic hexameter was used as a substitution in the heroic couplet .
several attempts were made in the 19th century to make the dactylic hexameter to english .
gerard manley hopkins wrote many of his poems in six-foot iambic and sprung rhythm lines .
in the 20th century a loose ballad with a strong medial pause was used by william butler yeats .
