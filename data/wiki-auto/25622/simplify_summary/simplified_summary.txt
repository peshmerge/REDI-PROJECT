richard bachman is a pen name that is used by stephen king .
at the beginning of stephen king 's career , the general view among publishers was that an author was limited to one book per year , since publishing more would not be unacceptable to the public .
king wanted to write under another name , in order to increase his publication without over-saturating the market for the king " brand " .
he convinced his publisher , signet books , to print these novels under a pseudonym .
in his introduction to " the bachman books , " king says that adopting the nom de plume bachman was also an attempt to make sense out of his career and try to answer the question of whether his success was due to talent or luck .
king concludes that he has yet to find an answer to the " talent versus luck " question , as he felt he was outed as bachman too early to know .
