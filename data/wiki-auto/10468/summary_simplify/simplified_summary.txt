emperor senka georgiezong [unk] 天 皇 , senka-tenno prefecture was the 28th emperor of japan , according to the traditional order of succession .
no certain dates can be assigned to this emperor 's life or reign .
he ruled the country during the early-6th century .
emperor senka was elderly at the time of his enthronement ; and his reign is said to have lasted for only three years .
senka 's contemporary title would not have been " tenno " , as most historians believe this title was not introduced until the reign of emperor tenmu and empress jito .

the actual sites of the graves of the early emperors remain problematic , according to some historians and archaeologists .
