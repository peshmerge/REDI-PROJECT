emperor ingyo gastoni -lrb- [unk] 天 皇 , ingyo-tenno prefecture -rrb- was the 19th emperor of japan , according to the traditional order of succession .
no certain dates can be assigned to this emperor 's life or reign .
historians consider details about the life of emperor ingyo to be possibly legendary .
he sat on the throne after hanzei died and ruled for forty years .
ingyo 's contemporary title would not have been " tenno " .
rather , it was presumably " sumeramikoto " or " amenoshita shiroshimesu okimi " 治 天 下 大 王 ¥ , meaning " the great king who rules all under heaven " .
ingyo might have been referred to as [unk] 大 王 or the " great king of yamato " .
he reformed the system of family and clan names , because many named themselves false names using higher ranked clan or family names .
the earliest recorded earthquake in japan was in 416 .
some scholars think ingyo was a king in the " book of song " .
according to " nihon shoki " , the king of the silla kingdom grieved very much when ingyo died .
to comfort the soul of ingyo , he presented japan 80 musicians .
the actual place of ingyo 's grave is not known .
