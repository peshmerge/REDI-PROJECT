the sidewise awards for alternate history were established in 1995 to recognize the best alternate history stories and novels of the year .
the awards take their name from the 1934 short story " sidewise in time " by murray leinster .
steven h silver , evelyn c . leeper , and robert b . schmunk created the award .
over the years , the number of judges has changed between three and eight , including judges in the uk and south africa .
each year , two awards are given , usually at the worldcon convention .
the short-form award is given to a work under 60,000 words in length .
the long-form award may be given to a work longer than 60,000 words , including both novels and series .
the judges may also choose to recognize an individual or work with a special achievement award .
