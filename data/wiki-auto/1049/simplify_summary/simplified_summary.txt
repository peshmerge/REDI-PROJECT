amateur is generally considered a person who pursues a particular activity or field of study independently from their source of income .
historically , the amateur was considered to be the ideal balance between pure intent , open mind and the interest or passion for a subject .
the ancient greek citizens would spend most of their time in other pursuits , but would compete according to their natural talents and abilities .
the " gentleman amateur " was a phenomenon in the gentry of great britain from the 17th century until even the 20th century .
with the start of the age of reason , people thinking more about how the world works around them , traded things like the cabinet of curiosities , and the writing of the book " the christian virtuoso " , started to shape the idea of the amateur gentleman .
the royal society in great britain was generally composed of these " gentleman amateurs " .
on the other hand , an amateur may be in a position to approach a subject with an open mindocation because there was no formal training .
an amateur who dabbles in a field out of interest rather than as a profession , or who possesses a general but superficial interest in any art or a branch of knowledge , is often referred to as a dilettante .
the only olympic events that still require participants to be amateurs are boxing , wrestling and figure skating .
many amateurs make valuable contributions in the field of computer programming through the open source movement .
amateur dramatics is the performance of plays or musical theatre , often to high standards , but lacks the budgets of professional west end or broadway performances .
astronomy , chemistry , history , linguistics , and the natural sciences are among the fields that have benefited from the activities of amateurs .
charles darwin and gregor mendel were amateur scientists who never held a position in their study .
william shakespeare and leonardo da vinci were considered amateur artists and autodidacts in their fields of study .
he started out by tinkering with a coherer and a spark coil as an amateur electrician .
