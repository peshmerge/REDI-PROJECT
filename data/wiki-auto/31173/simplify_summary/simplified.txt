telesto is a moon of saturn .
it was found by smith , reitsema , larson and fountain in 1980 from ground-based observations .
in the following months , several other apparitions were observed : s / 1980 s 24 , s / 1980 s 33 , and s / 1981 s 1 .
it was named after telesto in greek mythology .
it is also known as saturn xiii olympiads , tethys b , and saturn xiii olympiads .
telesto is co-orbital with tethys , living in tethys ' leading lagrangian point [mask] l federer .
this relationship was first identified by seidelmann in 1981 .
another moon , calypso , resides in the othercek lagrangian point of tethys , 60 degrees in the other direction from tethys .
the system has two more trojan moons .
the " cassini " probe landed a distant flyby of telesto on october 11,2005 .
the resulting images show that its surface is smooth , devoid of small impact craters .
