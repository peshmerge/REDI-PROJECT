a terabyte is a unit of information that can be used to measure data .
the prefix " tera " is the fourth power of 1000 .
the unit symbol for the terabyte is tb .
a related unit , the tebibyteyteyteyte guineas , using a binary prefix , is equal to 1024 bytes .
one terabyte is about 0.9095 tib .
despite the introduction of these standardized binary prefixes , the terabyte is still commonly used in some computer operating systems , primarily microsoft windows , to denote 1024 or 2 bytes for disk drive capacity .
