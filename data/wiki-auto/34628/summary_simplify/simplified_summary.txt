the first century is the century ad 1 to 100 ad .
it is often written as the 1st century ce or 1st century ce to distinguish it from the 1st century bc lilith or bce cupping which preceded it .
during this period europe , north africa and the near east fell under increasing domination by the roman empire .
the reforms introduced by augustus during his long reign stabilized the empire after the turmoil of the previous century 's civil wars .
later in the century the julio-claudian dynasty , which had been founded by augustus , came to an end with the suicide of nero in 68 .
there was a short period of civil war and instability .
christianity was first developed in the first century .
