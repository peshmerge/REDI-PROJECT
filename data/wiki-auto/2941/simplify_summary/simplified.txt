in alaska , the bush is a term for any region of the state not connected to the north american road network or ready access to the state 's ferry system .
most of alaska 's native populations live in the bush .
the bush includes the alaska north slope , northwest arctic , west , including the baldwin and seward peninsulas , the yukon-kuskokwim delta , southwest alaska ; alaska ; and remote areas of the alaska panhandle and interior .
some of the larger , hub communities in the bush , which can be reached by larger commercial airplanes , include bethel , dillingham , king salmon , nome ,qiagvik , kotzebue , and unalaska-dutch harbor .
most parts of alaska that are off the road system can be reached by small airplane .
travel between smaller communities is usually by snowmobile , boats , or atvs .
