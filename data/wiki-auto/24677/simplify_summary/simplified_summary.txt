the premier of queensland is the leader of the government of queensland , australia .
the premier is the leader of the party with a majority in the unicameral legislative assembly of queensland .
the members of the queensland parliament are appointed by the governor .
the constitution gives the premier certain powers , such as the power to assign roles thereof to assistant ministers thereof .
in practice , the premier 's power is got from two sources : command of a majority in the legislative assembly , and the premier 's role as chair of cabinet , determining the appointment and roles of ministers .
a re-elected government will be resworn , with changes to the ministry as determined by the premier .
the premier has an office in the executive annex of parliament house , brisbane , which is normally used while parliament is sitting .
before the 1890s , there was no political party system in queensland .
before the end of the first decade of the twentieth century , political parties were more akin to parliamentary factions , and were fluid , informal and disorganized by modern standards .
