loris is the common name for the strepsirrhine primates of the family lorisidae .
" loris " is one genus in this subfamily .
they live in tropical and woodland forests of india , sri lanka , and parts of southeast asia .
some lorises are almost entirely insectivorous , while others also include fruits , gums , leaves , and slugs in their diet .
before they do this , they bathe their young with allergenic saliva .
the family lorisidae is found in the infraorder lemuriformes and superfamily lorisoidea .
