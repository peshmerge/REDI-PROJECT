the lord of the rings is a book written by j . r . r . tolkien .
it was a sequel to " the hobbit " .
tolkien wrote the story between 1937 and 1949 .
the book is about the main antagonist of the story the dark lord sauron .
the story is from quiet beginnings in the shire , a hobbit land not unlike the english countryside .
tolkien wanted to be one book of a two-volume set , the other to be " the silmarillion " , but this idea was dismissed by his publisher .
it was published in three parts between 1954 and 1955 .
the novel is divided internally into six books , two per volume , with several appendices of background material included at the end .
" the lord of the rings " has been translated into 38 languages .
although a major work in itself , the story was only the last movement of a larger epic tolkien worked on since 1917 .
influences on this earlier work , and on the story of " the lord of the rings " , include philology , mythology , religion and the author 's distaste for the effects of industrialization , as well as earlier fantasy works and tolkien 's experiences in world war i .
" the lord of the rings " is considered to have had a great effect on modern fantasy .
the popular use of " the lord of the rings " has led to many references in popular culture .
" the lord of the rings " has inspired , and continues to inspire , artwork , music , movies and television , video games , board games , and later literature .
award-winning " the lord of the rings " have been made for radio , theatre , and film .
