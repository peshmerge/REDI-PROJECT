a gate is a place where the entry is enclosed by walls .
gates may prevent or control the entry or exit of individuals , or they may be merely decorative .
other terms for gate include " yett " and port .
the word " gat " comes from old norse " gat " , meaning road or path .
the moving part or parts of a gateway may be considered " doors " .
a gate may have a latch that can be raised and lowered to both open a gate or prevent it from swinging .
locks are also used on gates to increase the security .
larger gates can be used for a whole building , such as a castle or town .
real doors can also be considered gates when they are used to block entry as prevalent within a gatehouse .
today , many gate doors are opened by an automated gate operator .
