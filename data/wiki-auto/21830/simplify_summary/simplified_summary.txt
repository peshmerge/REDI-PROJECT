nature " can refer to the physical world , and to life in general .
although humans are part of nature , human activity is often seen as a separate category from other natural phenomena .
the word " nature " is derived from the latin word " natura " , meaning " naturalness " .
natura is a latin translation of the greek word " physis " φυσιςcinae .
the concept of nature as a whole , the physical universe , is one of several expansions of the original idea .
nature can refer to the general realm of living plants and animals , and in some cases the processes associated with inanimate objects – the way that particular types of things exist and change of their own accord , such as the weather and geology of the earth .
manufactured objects are generally not considered part of nature , unless qualified as , for example , " human nature " or " the whole of nature " .
depending on context , the term " natural " might also be distinguished from the supernatural .
