july is the seventh month of the year between june and august gregorian calendars and the fourth of seven months to have 31 days .
it was named by the roman senate in honor of roman general julius caesar .
" dog days " are considered to begin in early july in the northern hemisphere , when the hot sultry weather of summer usually starts .
july is the traditional period known as " fence month " .
the end of england 's high court of justice trinity term takes place on 31 july .
july is also the time in which the elections take place for the japanese house of councillors .
in ancient rome , the festival of poplifugia was celebrated on july 5 , and ludi apollinares was held on july 13 and for several days afterwards .
