the premier of new south wales is the leader of the government of new south wales , australia .
new south wales follows the westminster system , with a parliament of new south wales acting as the legislature .
the premier is appointed by the governor of new south wales .
before federation in 1901 the term " prime minister of new south wales " was also used .
the prime minister of australia has been the leader of the commonwealth of australia since 1901 .
the current premier is gladys berejiklian , the leader of the new south wales liberal party .
berejiklian replaced mike baird on 23 january 2017 , after baird resigned as premier .
