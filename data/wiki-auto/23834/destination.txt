Corrado di Suburra was born in Rome.
He was son of Benedetto di Suburra.
In 1127, della Suburra was made cardinal-bishop of Sabina.
Anastasius was elected pope in July 1153.
He is known for helping to settle a controversy between the Holy Roman Emperor and the Bishop of Magdeburg in Germany.
The Pantheon in Rome was restored while he was pope.
