a ganglion is a group of nerve cells in the central nervous system .
ganglia provide relay points and intermediary connections between different neurological structures in the body , such as the peripheral and central nervous systems .
in the autonomic nervous system , the ganglia are called postganglionic fibers .
the " basal ganglia " is a group of nuclei interconnected with the cerebral cortex , thalamus , and brainstem .
the terminologia anatomica suggests using the term basal nuclei instead of basal ganglia .
a pseudoganglion is a thickening of the main part of a nerve that has the appearance of a ganglion .
pseudoganglia are found in the teres minor muscle and radial nerve .
