the phanerozoic eon is the current geologic eon in the geologic time scale .
it began with the cambrian period when animals first developed hard shells preserved in the fossil record .
its name came from the ancient greek words " and " meaning " visible life " .
the time before the phanerozoic , called the " precambrian " , is now divided into the hadean , archaean and proterozoic eons .
