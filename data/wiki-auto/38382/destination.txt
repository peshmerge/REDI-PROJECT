This is a list of kings who ruled over the German territories of central Europe.
The kings reigned from the division of the Frankish Empire in 843 until the end of the German Empire in 1918.
It also includes the heads of the different German confederations after the collapse of the Holy Roman Empire in 1806.
Emperors are listed in bold.
Rival kings, anti-kings, and junior co-regents are listed in "italics".
